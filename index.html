Your Journey

How You'll Progress

01

Learn the Basics

Follow clear, structured lessons to grow your knowledge. Our expert teachers show you the proven path to mastering your instrument.

02

Apply Your Skills

Put new skills into practice in a fun, supportive environment. Play with other musicians, pick up pro tips, and make lasting friendships.

03

Play Your Songs

Play your favourite songs using the skills you've built. Once you're truly playing music you love, you'll never want to stop.

04

Master Music

Create and record your own music, teach others, and perform in public — whether in a band or as a soloist. The stage is yours.

Why Pathfinder

How Do You Want to Learn?

At this point, you've got 3 choices. Only one of them actually gets you to where you want to be.

😵

1. Teach Yourself

YouTube videos. Free tabs. Beginner books. This is how most people first try — and most find it overwhelming and slow. Without a clear path, progress stalls fast.

🎓

2. Casual Teacher

A poorly organised, unstructured lesson every other week might technically be lessons — but you can't expect mastery to come from that.

Best Choice
🎸

3. Pathfinder

Expert teachers with structured lessons built for you. Make rapid progress, meet great musicians, and actually have fun doing it. This is the only way to go.
